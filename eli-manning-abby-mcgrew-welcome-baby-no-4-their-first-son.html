Eli Manning, Abby McGrew Welcome Baby No. 4, Their First Son

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Party of six! Eli Manning and his wife, Abby McGrew, welcomed their fourth child on Super Bowl Sunday, February 3. Charles “Charlie” Elisha Manning is the couple’s first son.

The baby boy was born just after midnight, according to Newsday, joining the pair’s three daughters: Ava Frances, 7, Lucy Thomas, 5, and Caroline Olivia, 4.

The New York Giants quarterback, 38, opened up to Us Weekly about parenting his eldest back in June 2014. “She’s really funny,” he said at the time. “The first time I took her to some tennis courts she said, ‘Daddy, what’s that game?’ And I said, ‘That’s tennis.’ And she said, ‘I win at that game!’ And I just went, ‘Oh, goodness, gracious.’ She’s starting to get into some sports. She’s starting to play a little soccer.”

While Manning and McGrew, 35, only had two littles ones at home at the time, the two-time Super Bowl winner wasn’t certain they’d expand their family even further.

“It’s a possibility,” he said. “We’re enjoying the two, but I’m from a family of three and my wife’s from a family of three, so I think that’s always the number we had in our head. But we’ll have to see how it goes.”
