Pass the Mics DJ Cassidys Ultimate Party Playlist

August 2024 · 2 minute read

DJ Cassidy knows how to turn the party! Having spun for legendary celebrations like Jennifer Lopez’s 50th birthday, Beyoncé and Jay- Z’s 2008 wedding and two inauguration parties for Barack Obama, he knows how to get celebrities on the dance floor. Ahead of his first TV show airing on BET on Saturday, March 27, following the 52nd NAACP Image Awards, the iconic DJ shared his top songs guaranteed to get every party started.

In honor of his new show, Cassidy looks back on his digital series after having “passed the mic” to 113 legendary artists over the course of 81 iconic songs. He shares his top 25 ultimate “Pass the Mic” party playlist to set your party off right as the weather warms and the dance floors open.
