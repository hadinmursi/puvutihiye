Curt Smith Biography and Wiki

Curt Smith is a renowned English-American Singer, Songwriter, Musician, Record Producer and Actor who was born and brought-up in Bath, Somerset, England, United Kingdom.

He is widely known as the co-founding member of the pop rock band known as “Tears for Fears” alongside Roland Orzabal, his childhood friend. He played the bass guitar and co-wrote many of the songs of the “Tears for Fears” band.

He was also the lead vocal of many hits which include; “Mad World”, “Pale Shelter”, “Change”, “The Way You Are” and “Everybody Wants to Rule the World”.
