Who Is Randall Goosby? Wikipedia Bio

Randall Goosby is a young violinist who started his violin career at an early age. He has received many awards and is able to establish himself professionally.

He is represented by Harrison/Parrott and Primo Artists for Joint Worldwide General Management. His first album ‘Roots’, is a survey of music by black composers.

He became an Ambassador for the charity London Music Masters in 2020, a role that he will be mentoring and inspiring students in schools around the UK.

The violinist also played as part of “Concerts in Motion”, for elderly and homebound people. And a private concert, “Project: Music Heals Us” to Covid Victims.

On 14 October 2020, he signed with Decca Classics and announced an upcoming album of music by an African-American composer.

He has not been yet covered by Wikipedia. He has been honored as ‘Rising Star’ of the Stradivari Society. Soon Wikipedia will include his bio page for sure.

What is the Age of the Violinist?

The Violinist is 24 years of age. He was born in 1996. At the age of seven, he started learning violin and by nine he had made his debut performance.

He performed with an orchestra, the Jacksonville Symphony. At 13 he played with the New York Philharmonic at David Geffen Hall in New York’s Lincoln Center.

This performance gave him a standing ovation as per Times.  In 2018, he won the Young Concert Artists International Auditions and led becoming a YCAT artist in 2019.

Meet his Parents – His Ethnicity

Randall Goosby was born to an African-American father and a Korean mother. He is of mixed ethnicity.

His parents met each other in Japan. Her mother Jiji was raised there and his father, Ralph was teaching English as part of a post-collegiate program.

The couple moved to the US and got married, settling down in Jacksonville, Florida, raising a family. That was indeed a wonderful love story.

His mother is also a passionate music lover and dancer. Besides Randall Goosby, she bore another two kids a son and a daughter.
