Neville Page is a British-American creature and concept artist who has worked in cinema and television. He was born in England and reared in the cities of Manchester and Chicago, Illinois. He was influenced by science fiction, particularly Star Wars, as well as the work of makeup artist Rick Baker on the film An American Werewolf in London. Page went to Hollywood when he was 17 years old and began gaining experience as an actor. With honours, he graduated from the Art Center College of Design in 1990 with a degree in industrial design, and he went on to teach industrial design to students in Switzerland. Along with his business partner Scott Robertson, he concentrated his efforts on design consultancy. Page has worked as a concept designer on films such as Watchmen, Star Trek, TRON: Legacy, Cloverfield, and Avatar, among other projects.
