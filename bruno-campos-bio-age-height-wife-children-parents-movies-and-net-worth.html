Bruno Campos Biography

Bruno Campos is a Brazilian-American lawyer and former actor. He is best known for his roles as Dr. Quentin Costa on the award-winning television show Nip/Tuck, the voice of Prince Naveen in the animated film The Princess and the Frog, and Massimo in the Brazilian movie O Quatrilho. Campos attended law school in 2010, earning a J.D. in 2013, and currently works for the firm Covington & Burling LLP in New York City.

Bruno Campos Actor

As an actor, in 1995, Bruno starred in the Brazilian film O Quatrilho which was nominated for Best Foreign Language Film at the Academy Awards. In the same year, he was cast as the male lead at The Goodman Theatre in “All’s Well That Ends Well”, directed by Mary Zimmerman.

He gained fame in the American platform for his roles as Diego on the NBC sitcom Jesse, starring Christina Applegate; as attorney Joe Rivera in the Showtime series Leap Years; and for a recurring guest role as Dr. Eddie Dorset on ER.

He joined the cast of Nip/Tuck in 2004, playing the role of plastic surgeon Quentin Costa. In 2010, Bruno made a guest appearance in Private Practice. Campos also had a portrayed Charlie Casey recurring role, in the first season of Royal Pains, the ex-husband of one of the lead characters, Jill Casey.

He also lent his voice to the Disney film, the princess, and the frog’s character Prince Naveen in the Disney film which was released in 2009 and went on to be nominated for three Academy Awards.

Bruno Campos Lawyer

Campos quit the entertainment industry in the early 2010s and enrolled in law school. He earned a J. D. from the University of Michigan in 2013. While in law school, he held an internship with judge Avern Cohn of the US District Court for the Eastern District of Michigan.

After graduating he passed the bar in New York. Based on Campos’ contributions to reports on securities law enforcement generated by the law firm Morgan, Lewis & Bockius, he has been with the New York firm since 2015.

According to his job profile, Campos is “part of a team that represents clients in healthcare, environmental and toxic tort, patent and trademark, securities, and product liability cases”, as well as “guid[ing] clients through sensitive corporate investigations and international arbitration, and defend[ing] clients in criminal matters.”
