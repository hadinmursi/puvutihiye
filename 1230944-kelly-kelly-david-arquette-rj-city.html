Kelly Kelly Gets Married

Kelly Kelly name will be getting a little name variety now as the former WWE Diva announced that she’s tied the knot. Kelly, real name Barbie Blank, announced via Instagram yesterday that she and fiance Joe Coba exchanged vows to become husband and wife.

“It’s you and me forever baby!,” Kelly wrote. “Joe Coba, you truly are my whole world, lover, and best friend. I cannot wait to walk with you hand and hand thru life. And honestly it’s the best feeling in the world to call you my husband! ‘Love is passion, obsession, someone you can’t live without. Find someone you can love like crazy and who will love you the same way back.'”

WWE.com wrote the following on its website:

“Kelly Kelly (a.k.a. Barbara “Barbie” Blank Coba) tied the knot this past weekend! The former Divas Champion released a beautiful picture of her wedding on social media yesterday. WWE.com wishes the happy couple a wonderful future together.”

We here at WrestleZone would like to congratulate Joe and Barbara on their new journey together!
