Former White House intern Mary Caitrin Mahoney, also known as Mary Mohane, was shot and killed in a coffee shop. She was said to be going to testify at the White House regarding sexual harassment.

Former White House intern Mary Caitrin Mohane, 25, was slain along with two coworkers (Emory Allen Evans, 25, and Aaron David Goodrich, 18) during a heist at a Georgetown Starbucks on July 6, 1997. Carl Derek Havord Cooper (29) of Washington was arrested and accused of the killings in March 1999.

What Happened To Mary Mohane? Age At Death Revealed

Mary Mohane, aged 25, who was a former intern at the White House during President Bill Clinton’s term was killed in the course of a robbery along with two other employees during which nothing was taken.

Murderer Carl Derek Havord Cooper was sentenced to life in prison without the possibility of release on April 26, 2000, after entering a guilty plea. He went to Starbucks, knowing that the receipts over the July 4 weekend would net him a tidy profit. He entered after the store was closed, brandished a.38, and ordered all three Starbucks employees into the back room.

Cooper first shot her, then the other two employees. He fled empty-handed, fearful that the gunfire would draw police notice. As tragic as these three fatalities were, they were just the result of a heist gone awry.

Mary Mohane And Bill Clinton’s Affair

Mary Mohane and Bill Clinton’s affair rumor was a controversial matter and while many believed that it was a conspiracy theory, some sources claim that there’s no reason why Mahoney, out of all the Clinton White House interns, could be the sole victim of a Clinton-directed assassination. Nevertheless, as per the Clinton Body Count conspiracy theory, Bill has had people assassinated.

So far, no evidence of Mary Mohane’s romance with Bill Clinton has been discovered. In fact, contrary to popular belief, few interns work in the West Wing of the White House or have any interaction with the President, according to some sources. The majority of interns only receive a single quick handshake or group photo with the chef. As a result, the affair between an intern and the President was rather problematic in this circumstance.

Why Was Former Intern At White House, Mary Mohane Murdered?

Official sources have not confirmed the reason given for Mahoney’s death, that she was due to speak about sexual harassment in the White House.

This explanation appears to have sprung from a tip from Newsweek’s Mike Isikoff, just before the Monica Lewinsky scandal broke, that a “former White House worker” beginning with the letter “M” was due to speak about her affair with Clinton. Monica Lewinsky, not Mary Mahoney, was the “staffer” in question.

According to conspiracy theorists, White House hitmen went out at random and shot the first female ex-intern whose name started with the letter “M.”
