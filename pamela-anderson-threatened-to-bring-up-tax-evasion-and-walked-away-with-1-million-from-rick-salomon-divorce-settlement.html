Pamela Anderson Walked Away With A Cool $1 Million After Her Second Divorce To Rick Salomon

Back in 2014, Forbes revealed that Pamela Anderson divorced Rick Salomon again, getting $1 million from the settlement. According to the report, taxes were an issue and Anderson was "making noise" over an alleged tax evasion issue.

Forbes writes, "Taxes were an issue, and observers say she had been making noises about alleged tax evasion. TMZ says the nasty battle came to an end with Anderson up by $1 million. Saloman apparently was under pressure over tax claims, with Anderson questioning his taxes."

The previous marriage between the two was annulled after just two months. They married for a second time in 2013, but once again, it was short-lived as they finalized the divorce in 2014. At that point, it was Anderson's third failed marriage, the others coming alongside Tommy Lee and Kid Rock.

Pamela Anderson Allegedly Ended Her Marriage To Rick Saloman With An Email

Discussing her time with Tommy Lee, Pamela Anderson didn't hesitate to call him the love of her life looking back. “There was Tommy and then there was nobody else. He was the love of my life. We had a wild and crazy beginnings that was too much for both of us.”

“It really was love at first sight,” says Anderson. “I only knew him four days before I married him.”

It seems like Rick Salomon didn't get that type of treatment from his ex, as according to The Mirror,Anderson sent a savage email to let him know the marriage was over.

The publication wrote, "In 2015, TMZ acquired court documents that showed she'd filed to divorce Rick for the third time. Amid a flurry of speculation about what had brought about their final split, TMZ reported that she'd sent him an email detailing her grievances."

"Sources told the online publication that the email, sent February 2015, contained a line that read: "We are not compatible sexually. I'm frustrated and bored. The actress's rep told at the time: "Pamela is moving on with her life, spending time with her family, working on numerous projects including her foundation, The Pamela Anderson Foundation."

That wouldn't be the last of it for Anderson and her divorces. She remarried in 2020, tying the knot with Dan Hayhurst. However, two years later, in 2022, it was announced that the two were going their own ways.

Pamela Anderson Called Her Recent Divorce With Dan Hayhurst A "Disaster"

Pamela Anderson detailed her marriage with Vanity Fair, saying her short marriage ended up being a disaster.

Anderson called the marriage her very worst out of the others in the past. "It ended up being a disaster." She continues, "It was worse than any of them. And I realized, 'Oh my God, I'm caught up in this whole thing, what am I doing?' It just truck me, this is another disaster. ... I was paying for everything, cooking, cleaning for everybody. And so I just got out of it as soon as I could."

Anderson concluded that she's content with being on her own for the time being.

"I've learned to try and do this without a life preserver, without having anyone around me to console me. I have to console myself. That's been the hardest part of the journey — to go, I'm okay on my own ... with my dogs. I need my dogs. I can't be that alone."

Anderson continues, "I had this wild, bumpy life and met a lot of interesting people along the way and had a lot of interesting love affairs. But I feel like my life is more romantic now than ever. I have dinner for one or with my dogs. I'm the same person with or without somebody. Rose petals in the bathtub, making my own rose oils, making my own candles. I'm kind of crafty all of a sudden."

Anderson is more than okay on her own nowadays, with a net worth of $20 million.
