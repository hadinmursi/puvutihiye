Busy Philipps, Evan Rachel Wood Found Out They Were Pregnant On Same D

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Who knew!? In a hilariously unexpected moment at a special screening for A Case of You in Los Angeles last night, costars Busy Philipps and Evan Rachel Wood realized they had one big thing in common — they found out they were pregnant on the very same day! It all went down during a panel for their new film at the Regal Theatre Stadium.

During the panel, Philipps and Wood recalled how they were both pregnant during filming while actor Justin Long — who wrote the screenplay — was simultaneously getting over a recent breakup.

"I got pregnant October 10th," Philipps, 34, revealed. "Oh my God! I swear to God that's the day that I got pregnant," Wood chimed in. Philipps' response? "Shut up!"

The Cougar Town actress welcomed her second daughter, Cricket, on July 2 with husband Marc Silverstein. The couple are already parents to Birdie, 5. Wood, 26, gave birth to a baby boy with hubby Jamie Bell just weeks later on July 30.

Though a huge coincidence, Philipps swears that Wood's character Birdie in the film wasn't named after her own daughter. "It was something else. But, I do remember reading it — and because we are friends with Justin outside of this movie we did ask him, my husband asked him," she told Us Weekly on the red carpet.

As for Cricket — who she lovingly calls "Crickles" — she's reaching milestones each day. "She just started rolling from her tummy to her back, so that's exciting," Philipps said of her 3-month-old to Us. "She laughs really hard now."

"But, when I ask her, for instance, to get like a diaper cloth or a burp cloth or a diaper for me, she says, 'Mom, that's boring and I don't want to do that.'"
