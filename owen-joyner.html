Biography Timeline

Owen Joyner is an American actor known for his roles in several popular television shows. Here is a detailed biography timeline of Owen Joyner:

  • July 19, 2000: Owen Patrick Joyner was born in Norman, Oklahoma, United States.
  • 2013: Joyner begins his acting career with a guest role on the “See Dad Run” television show.
  • 2015: Joyner lands a recurring role on the Nickelodeon television show “100 Things to Do Before High School”.
  • 2017: Joyner stars in the Nickelodeon television movie, “Legends of the Hidden Temple”.
  • 2018: Joyner joins the cast of the Netflix series, “Julie and the Phantoms”, in the lead role of “Julie’s” love interest, “Alex”.
  • 2019: Joyner receives critical acclaim for his performance in “Julie and the Phantoms” and is nominated for a Young Artist Award for Best Leading Young Actor in a T.V. Series.
  • 2020: Joyner is cast in a recurring role on the Disney+ series “The Right Stuff”.
