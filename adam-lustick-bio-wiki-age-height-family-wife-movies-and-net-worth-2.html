Adam Lustick Biography

Adam Lustick is an American actor and writer. He co-founded the critically acclaimed sketch group Harvard Sailing Team and was named a Comic to Watch by Variety in 2014, Comedy Central in 2015, and Time Out LA in 2016. Veep, Highston, Casual, The Office, Silicon Valley, The Comedians, Adam Ruins Everything, and the upcoming films Little Bitches and Dirty 30 are among his acting credits.

Lustick began his career as a producer, writer, and featured player on MTV’s reboot of Punk’d, and then went on to create, executive produce, and star in Sports Talkers, his own sports talk show for Fusion. He’s also written a pilot for Adult Swim starring Jason Alexander. Both Adam and Billy Scafuri are members of the sketch group Harvard Sailing Team and have their own podcast, ‘No Joke with Billy Scafuri.’ In each episode, the two hosts choose and discuss a topic suggested by Twitter users. Since 2016 when the podcast has had over 200 episodes with guests such as Ben Schwartz, Milana Vayntrub, and more.
