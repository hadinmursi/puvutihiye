15 Things Everyone Forgot About Katy Perry And Russell Brand's Relationship

September 2024 · 8 minute read

Katy Perry may be engaged to hunky Orlando Bloom now, but nearly 10 years ago, she was saying “I do” to a very different Brit. Katy Perry and comedian, Russell Brand, were definitely an unlikely duo, but their whirlwind relationship captivated the media...for better or worse.

Katy Perry and Russell Brand’s relationship is perfectly summed up with Katy’s own song, “Hot N Cold.” They were super hot and heavy until they weren’t. Even though their relationship only lasted a short time, a lot of confusing and complicated stuff went down that we still can’t completely figure out.

No doubt we are all very happy Katy Perry has found love again, but we can’t help but reflect back on one of her more publicized relationships and breakups.

15 Katy Perry And Russell Brand Met At The 2008 VMAs

Katy Perry and Russell Brand first met in 2008, just as both of their careers were really starting to take off. Their paths crossed at the MTV  VMAs, which were being hosted by Russell. Brand and his Get Him to the Greek director approached Katy and Pink about shooting some celebrity cameos for the comedy film, according to Heavy.

Their romantic relationship didn’t begin until the next year - that was when they reconnected at the 2009 VMAs.

14 Katy Perry Was Almost In Russell Brand’s Film ‘Get Him To The Greek’

Katy Perry accepted the Get Him to the Greek cameo offer she received from Russell Brand and director, Nick Stoller. However, the director decided to cut her part from the movie before it was released in theaters.

“If you saw me and Russell making out, although it’d be cute to, ’cause that was the first moment we met, ‘There’s Russell Brand in the movie,’ not Aldous Snow, and they want it to be Aldous Snow, [so it’s] completely understandable,” Katy explained to MTV.

13 Russell Proposed To Katy Just Three Months Into Their Relationship

Russell Brand popped the big question to Katy Perry just three months after they began their exotic relationship. On New Year’s Eve 2009, Russell proposed to Katy while they were on vacation in India.

According to E! News, the exciting event took place in a tent at the foot of a mountain in Jaipur, India and was blessed by a “love guru.”

12 Katy Perry And Russell Brand Got Married In A Hindu Ceremony

On October 23, 2010, Katy and Russell were officially married in a Hindu ceremony. The couple kept most of the details from their wedding a secret, but it has since been revealed that Katy wore a beautiful grey Elie Saab haute couture wedding dress. There were only 80 guests in attendance, according to Popsugar.

11 Their Wedding Was Extravagant And Strange

Katy Perry and Russell Brand’s wedding ceremony and celebrations were not what someone would typically expect from a Hollywood couple.

According to Popsugar, Russell’s big entrance on their special day involved an elephant ride, along with traditions from Hindu, South Asian and Christian faiths. Guests enjoyed several performances, shopping sprees, and safari trips. Overall, their unique wedding was described as “very private and spiritual.”

10 Russell Brand Broke Up With Katy Perry By Text Message

Katy and Russell’s wedding bliss didn’t last for long, though, because just a year later, Russell Brand filed for divorce, citing “irreconcilable differences.”

A year after their divorce was finalized, Katy Perry opened up to Vogue, according to Page Six. She talked about Russell and the divorce.

She stated, “He’s a very smart man, a magical man and I was in love with him when I married him. Let’s just say I haven’t heard from him since he texted me saying he was divorcing me December 31, 2011.”

9 Russell And Katy’s Conflicting Schedules Caused Their Divorce

Shortly after news of their split broke, both Katy Perry and Russell Brand blamed their hectic schedule for the divorce. In an interview on The Howard Stern Show, according to Who, Russell explained, “We did work it out, she’s happy, I’m happy. I was really, really in love with her, but it was difficult to see each other, it mostly didn't work for practical reasons.”

Katy shared those same sentiments in her 2012 documentary, Katy Perry: Part of Me, confirming that scheduling conflicts damaged their relationship.

8 But Russell Brand Likely Had Other Reasons For The Divorce

After the shock of the couple’s divorce wore off, Russell Brand started sharing more insights into possible reasons that he filed for divorce from the pop star.

In his documentary BRAND: A Second Coming, Russell described his life with Katy in a scathing way: “I'm associated with the very thing I detest: vapid, vacuous, plastic, constructed, mindless celebrity. That's the very sea we're swimming in, 'Oh, who's he?' ... 'He's married to Katy Perry.’”

A visit to Africa changed Russell Brand’s spiritual view on life and how he viewed his marriage with Katy Perry.

7 After The Divorce, Katy Perry Fell Into A Deep Depression

Katy Perry’s divorce took a huge toll on her mental health. In an interview with Vogue India, Katy revealed just how depressed she became after her breakup with Russell Brand.

“I became depressed and I did not want to get out of bed. In the past, I had been able to overcome it, but this time something happened that made me fall down too many flights of stairs. I had to really go on a mental health journey.”

6 The Pop Star Wrote A Song About Her Divorce From Russell Brand

As part of her healing, Katy Perry wrote a song documenting her struggle to overcome the pain and heartache that came with her divorce. Her song, “By the Grace of God,” has lyrics that pointed toward some really dark thoughts, according to Who.

“That song is evident of how tough it really was at a certain point. I asked myself, 'Do I want to endure? Should I continue living,’” Katy confessed, according to Who.

5 Katy Perry Kept The Cat She Owned With Russell Brand

Though Katy and Russell didn’t share any children together, they did adopt a cat. The feline was given the name Rusty, as a combination of their names.

After the divorce, Katy kept the cat as her own and changed its name. “I still have the cat, but I’ve changed the name to Monkey,” Katy admitted in an interview, according to Fame10.

4 Katy Claimed Russell Was Very Controlling During The Relationship

News of Katy Perry and Russell Brand’s divorce came as somewhat of a surprise to fans... because no one really got a look at what was happening inside of the relationship. Katy revealed some of the bad stuff in an interview with Vogue.

“At first when I met him, he wanted an equal... He didn’t like the atmosphere of me being the boss on tour. So that was really hurtful, and it was very controlling, which was upsetting.”

3 Russell Brand Wasn’t Very Supportive Of Katy’s Career

Russell Brand and Katy Perry’s levels of fame were vastly different, and Katy’s fame kept getting greater and greater.

“She had so many things going on this year and there were so many times he wasn’t there,” an Us Weekly source shared. “He told her she wasn’t funny [when she hosted SNL] and he was totally uninterested [when he sat front row at her last concert]. He seemed annoyed he was there. I don’t think he sat through one whole song.”

2 And He Mad Some Awful Jokes About His Relationship With Katy

Russell Brand is definitely known for having a wild comedic act, but the jokes he made about Katy Perry after their divorce went a little too far. According to NY Daily News, during an interview on The Howard Stern Show, Russell said Katy wasn’t “freaky enough” for him.

He described his love life with Katy as “being on a diet of rice” and his after-divorce life as “being let loose in Willy Wonka's chocolate factory.”

1 But Now, Russell Brand Hopes To Make Amends With Katy Perry Years After Their Divorce

A lot has changed for Russell Brand since his divorce from Katy Perry in 2012. He is now married and he has become a father. Years after their divorce, Russell hoped to make amends with the pop star.

After receiving a blessing from his wife and Katy’s fiancé, Orlando Bloom, Russell set out on meeting up with Katy. “He thinks it is important to clear the air. He wants to say sorry — in particular for the way it ended which was mostly his doing,” shared a source for The Sun.
