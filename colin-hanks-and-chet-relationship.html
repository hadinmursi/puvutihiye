What Does Colin Hanks Think About His Brother Chet?

August 2024 · 5 minute read

Tom Hanks is not only known as one of the very best actors, but it is also believed that he is a joy to work with. Given his status, there must've been huge pressure for Colin and Chet growing up. Colin has gone on to a stellar career in Hollywood, while it seems like Chet might still be finding his way.

He has been in the headlines for all the wrong reasons in the past, like getting slapped with a lawsuit on his birthday, along with getting cut off by his dad financially.

Given his different lifestyle, fans are wondering where he stands nowadays with his brother Colin. Here's what we know about the two.

What Is Colin Hanks And Chet's Relationship Like?

Although Chet turned out to be a little different in comparison to his father, he has revealed that despite his fame, he grew up with quite the normal childhood.

However, Chet did admit things were different at times, like for example, how he would only make friends with those interested in meeting his father.

“Like, growing up, people would want to become friends with me so they could meet my dad,” he alleged. "And they didn't care about me at all. So that's just … Well, that goes along with it.”

Alongside Us Magazine, Chet would further reveal that no matter what he did, he would always be known as Tom's son.

“I love my dad, he's a great man, he's a great actor, so I'm in a good position, you know what I mean? If I invented a cure for cancer tomorrow, in the article it would say, 'Tom Hanks' Son Invents The Cure For Cancer,' you know what I mean? It's inevitable.”

Without a doubt, his brother Colin turned out to be different, aligning more with his dad in terms of career paths. The family typically keeps quiet about Chet, though in 2015, Colin would briefly discuss their relationship.

Colin Hanks Doesn't Act Like Chet Doesn't Exist

Unlike some of the other family members, Colin Hanks doesn't ignore his brother. He answered a question about their relationship on the 'Sam Roberts Show', briefly discussing the dynamic.

Yes, they are very different, but that doesn't mean that Colin steers away from his brother. Instead, during the 2015 interview, he revealed that he wants what's best for his brother, giving him pieces of advice along the way.

"Do you have a relationship with your brother," Sam Roberts asked. "Yea, Of course," Colin responds. "We have a big age gap and obviously he's doing his own thing. He has an incredible work ethic, I want him to follow his passion and do what he loves to do."

Colin would then be asked if he gives his brother advice, "Of course, everybody gives their younger brother advice. But at the same time, he's a grown man that makes his own decisions." Colin would also state that he would tell his brother to think twice when it came to certain decisions he was making.

On YouTube, fans praised for Colin's answer.

"Obviously his brother is a bit cringe, but it's good to see Colin be a loving brother & not just throw him under the bus like the interviewer was probably trying to get at."

Fans would also note that the two grew up in different time periods, "Chet was born when Tom had become a stable actor, won Oscars and became one of the most known actor in Hollywood. He also have parents with a stable relationship and money to spend on anything he wants. It says a lot how ones childhood affect how they are as an adult."

Yes, the two clearly have a different relationship with their father, with fans usually stating that Colin is very similar to his dad.

Colin Hanks And Chet Have A Different Relationship With Their Dad Tom

Colin and Tom have a different relationship and that could be due to the fact that he was born earlier, when father Hanks wasn't as accomplished. Speaking alongside Hollywood Life, Colin would reveal that his dad had some great advice for his at a young age, which was that life is long. "That’s a hard one! I think, if there was anything, it’s while it may go fast in the rearview mirror and it may feel like it’s gone too quick, or sometimes maybe it feels like it goes to painstakingly slow, life is long, and there’s a lot of time to be able to do the things that you want to do and the things that you need to do."

"That idea of just taking your time, doing the work and really putting the time in, for whatever it may be, whether it’s your career, your family or your friendships, just putting that plan in and making that effort, makes all the difference.”

Colin continues to be busy on the small screen with new projects such as 'The Offer' and 'A Friend of the Family'. As for Chet, he continues to active on Instagram, with his own workout program, along with the label of music producer on his profile.
