Lina Lansberg, aged 40, is slightly older than her to-be husband Akira Corassani. Let’s get to know their age gap below.

Lina Lansberg, a Swedish MMA, takes the spot 11 in the UFC women’s bantamweight rankings as of November 2021.

Known by the alias ‘Elbow Queen’, this Swedish athlete represents Redline Training Center and has founded Lbowacademy.

Lina Lansberg has managed to land on the headlines for defending Ronda Rousey from the mean comments of Julianna Peña, who labeled Rousey as a sellout for taking an exit from MMA for pro wrestling. UFC: Lina Lansberg Husband Akira Corassani Age Gap

Lina Lansberg and her future husband Akira Corassani has an age gap of around only one year.

And, guess what, Akira is the younger one and Lina is the older one in a relationship as she is aged 40 years old whereas Akira is 39 at present. Actually, both share the same birth year but their birth month and date distinguished their real age. So, to be exact, the March-born Lina, who recently turned 40, has a difference of five months and 12 days as her soon-to-be husband was born on August 27 in Lund, Sweden.

Regarding the professional details of her husband-to-be, Akira currently is involved as a Head coach and co-founder of Redline Training Center. He gives training classes for kids, beginners, intermediates, and professionals. Some of his training includes MMA, Boxing, Muay Thai, NoGi, and many more.

Likewise, the partner of Lansberg, Akira Corassani is also a Former UFC fighter and TUF 14 contestant and he believes himself as a doer of many things. As of now, the retired Iranian-Swedish mixed martial artist Corassani also is engaged as a coach to his girlfriend Lina Lansberg.

What is Lina Lansberg Net Worth? Her Salary and Earnings

Lina Lansberg has an estimated net worth ranging from $200,000 to $5 million as per the multiple online sources.
