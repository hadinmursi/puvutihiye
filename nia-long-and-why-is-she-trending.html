Who is Ime Udoka's fiancee Nia Long?

August 2024 · 2 minute read

IME UDOKA's fiancee is Hollywood pop icon and actress star Nia Long.

The Brooklyn native is best known for her role in the Fresh Prince Of Bel Air.

Who is Nia Long?

Nia Long, is an actress known as one Hollywood's biggest pop icons.

Long, who as born on October 30, 1970. has been acting since the late 80s.

She is most known for her role as Lisa Wilks on The Fresh Prince Of Bel-Air from 1991-1995.

A few years later she had a recurring role on the crime show Third Watch, where she played Sasha Monroe.

Since then she has gone on to star in Big Shots, the Cleveland Show, Empire and NCIS: Los Angeles.

Her movies include Look Both Ways (2022), The Single Moms Club (2014) and Big Momma's House (2000 and 2006).

Throughout her career, Long has won three National Association for the Advancement of Colored People awards.

She won the best actress in a drama series back-to-back for her role in Third Watch.

Long also won best actress in a motion picture for her role in The Best Man.

And she has also appeared in music videos, including Kayne West's video for his song Touch The Sky.

As of 2022, Long's net worth is $6million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

When did Nia Long and Ime Udoka get married?

Long is not married but she is thought to still be engaged to long-time boyfriend and former NBA player Ime Udoka.

The two went public back in 2010 before getting engaged in 2015.

Udoka is the head coach of the Boston Celtics, following a seven year NBA career.

In 2020, Long went on ESSENCE’s “Yes, Girl!” podcast where she opened up about her feelings on marriage.

She revealed: "Love is complicated, and then you start a marriage, which I’ve never been married.

"And there’s a reason for that because I don’t know that I need to say ‘I do' to prove to you that I love you.

"But also, what does marriage really [mean]… that you own me now and I own you? I don’t get that part."

How many children does Nia Long have?

Nia and Imi have raised two children together.

Long had her first son, Massai Zhivago Dorsey II, with ex-boyfriend Massai Z. Dorsey back in 2000.
