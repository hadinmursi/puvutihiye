The Important Question Travis Kelce Asked Taylor Swift At Super Bowl After Party As They Continue To

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift flew back from Tokyo just in time to support Travis Kelce at the Super Bowl.
  • Travis Kelce was visibly thrilled to have Taylor Swift by his side during his career milestone and used the occasion to ask her a special question.
  • Fans speculate that Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's relationship is getting serious as they share their plans for after the Super Bowl.

Taylor Swift has been a regular feature in Kansas City Chiefs' games for a few months now, so why would the Super Bowl be an exception? She wouldn't miss out on supporting Travis Kelce for anything. Not even four tour dates in Tokyo could keep her away.

As soon as she was done with her concerts on the other side of the world, she took a flight back to the States, arriving just in time.

In fact, he used one of her songs to ask her a very important question.

Travis Kelce's Question To Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift was, undoubtedly, one of the stars of the Super Bowl. And she didn't even perform. She was there with her family and close friends to support Travis Kelce, and when the Kansas City Chiefs won, she was the one Travis wanted to celebrate with the most.

The couple was seen being practically glued together as soon as they were allowed next to each other, and several videos came out later of them at the after party, looking happier than ever and all over each other.

At one point, the DJ started playing remixes of Taylor Swift's songs, presumably at Travis' request, and the whole party lost their minds. The couple danced together to "Love Story", pointing at each other when the song said "You'll be the prince and I'll be the princess."

Another one of Taylor's songs that played was "You Belong With Me", and Travis hyped the crowd and danced wildly to it. And then he changed the lyrics to ask a question.

Not the one everyone was expecting, but an important one nonetheless. With a huge, euphoric smile, instead of "you belong with me" he yelled "are you in love with me?"

Unfortunately for fans, Taylor wasn't shown in the video, but judging by their interactions, Travis already knows the answer.

Taylor And Travis Might Not Be Engaged, But It's Getting Serious

A lot of people were expecting, or hoping, that Travis Kelce would use the Super Bowl as a setting for a proposal, but much like he anticipated before the match, his focus was solely on winning.

However, after everything the couple have gone through in the short time they've been together (countless Chiefs games, an impromptu trip to Buenos Aires, wearing jewelry with their initials), fans are sure that things are getting serious between them.

Now that the Super Bowl is over, Travis finally has time off, and has reportedly chosen to spend it with Taylor. He couldn't accompany her much in the past few months, because he couldn't really leave the country during the season, so he wasn't able cheer her on as much as she did him. But that's about to change.

Taylor Swift is now embarking on another leg of her Eras tour, and Travis will be joining her on the journey. "After the Super Bowl, Travis will be joining Taylor on her tour and traveling with her," a source shared a few days ago. "They are happy and looking forward to spending more time together."

No one knows where their relationship will go from there, but it seems they are on the right track for now.
