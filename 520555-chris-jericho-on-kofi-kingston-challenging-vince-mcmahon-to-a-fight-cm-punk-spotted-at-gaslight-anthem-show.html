Chris Jericho On Kofi Kingston Challenging Vince McMahon To A Fight, CM Punk Spotted At Gaslight Ant

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Kofi Kingston Challenges Vince McMahon

WWE Superstar and Fozzy frontman Chris Jericho has been making rounds promoting his new book, The Best In The World… At What I Have No Idea, which was released in stores and online last Tuesday. 

Jericho has been talking about several different stories that made the book, from Kid Rock’s reaction to a Wrestlemania match to Vince McMahon’s attempt to use a DVD player. One of the more interesting stories in the book is a recollection of a heated confrontation between WWE Superstar Kofi Kingston and WWE Chairman Vince McMahon. 

Jericho’s story starts with a comment Vince made to Kofi on a plane ride about maybe someday him [Kofi] getting over. Kofi took offense to it and looked angry, so Jericho caught up with him later and told him to challenge Vince to a fight. Jericho says he knew Vince wasn’t joking, so Kofi went to Vince and asked if he had a problem. Kofi and Vince apparently traded insults and went back and forth until it came to a physical altercation, with Vince even trying to take Kofi down by the legs. The two wrestled until they were pulled apart, and Vince and Kofi ended up laughing off the incident after it was over, and he was actually happy Kofi made a stand and brought the issue to him.
