'Summer House' Lindsay Hubbard Announces Pregnancy With Unknown New Boyfriend

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Highlights

  • Lindsay Hubbard announces pregnancy on Instagram, excited for her first baby with new boyfriend.
  • Hubbard shares her journey and emotions after a tough year following her split from Carl Radke.
  • Due date is in the Holiday season 2024, fans eagerly await more details about her boyfriend.

On July 5, Summer House star Lindsay Hubbard announced to the world on her Instagram that she’s expecting her first baby with her new boyfriend. She went through a tough heartbreak when Carl Radke completely blindsided her by calling off their wedding but has thankfully moved on from that horrible moment.

She is now expecting a baby later this year and, judging by her Instagram posts, she couldn't be happier. Lindsay and her boyfriend, who fans know nothing about, are very excited about starting the next chapter of their lives, and fans will surely learn more about their pregnancy as time goes on.

Lindsay Hubbard Announced Her Pregnancy

She was over the moon and posted several photos of herself cradling her pregnant belly, as well as one picture of her positive pregnancy test and one where the father’s hands can be seen, even though she still hasn’t revealed her boyfriend’s identity.

“Trigger Warning: This post contains more fireworks than the 4th of July! WE’RE PREGNANT!!!!!” her post began. “I truly believe the universe has a bigger plan and this is it! The second I had a feeling, I took a @clearblue Early Digital Pregnancy Test and getting a clear result, in words, made the next steps sooo much easier.”

Regarding her due date, she revealed “My boyfriend and I are beyond excited to welcome our little #HubbCub this Holiday season 2024!!”

Shortly after, she made another post where she delved into her feelings surrounding her pregnancy after going through a tough time in the past year.

“I woke up this morning feeling so blessed and grateful for this journey,” Lindsay wrote. “But most of all, I’m relieved and excited to finally be able to celebrate openly, and with the world.”

“It has been extremely difficult to hide my excitement in secrecy, and now that I’m able to share my happiness and joy, I feel like a weight has been lifted (even though I’m actually gaining weight by the day lol)! Our little cub is already so so loved! Thank you for all of the sweet comments, love, and support! We are truly over the moon right now!”

Lindsay Hubbard's Failed Engagement

Lindsay Hubbard has had a tough year following her split from Carl Radke. He was the one who called out the engagement, turning her life upside down, according to Lindsay herself.

"I was completely blindsided,” she said to Us Weekly last year. “He blew up my entire life. I was crying, I was angry, and I had to find closure on my own. Now I’m at a place where I feel really good about moving on."

Her anger at the way Carl called off the engagement made it easier for Lindsay to move on.

"The first month, I allowed myself to feel all of the feels: go through the sadness, the pain, the whiplash. I process [things] by talking about my emotions, and my friends allowed me that space," she said. "I was able to regain my strength and [the] confidence of like, OK, I can do this. The way that Carl handled everything was such an absolute betrayal of my trust, and when that happens, it makes it easier to pick yourself up and put yourself out there again."

She clearly managed to heal and move on, and is now incredibly excited for the next phase of her life.
