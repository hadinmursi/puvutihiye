Yano Anaya Married Life With Wife Selena Anduze And Family

Actor-producer Yano Anaya is married to his wife Selena Anduze, who is an American actress. The couple has been together for over a decade now.

Along with being an American actor, Yano is also a Body Transformation Expert and Exercise Educator. The actor is well-known for his work as Grover Dill in A Christmas Story and was also one of the paperboys in the film Better Off Dead.

Yano has also performed all voice-over dialogue for the lead paperboy in Better Off Dead. Working in the entertainment field, he starred alongside Dylan McDermott and Dean Stockwell in the hit indie film, The Blue Iguana. Anaya has done some notable work, as seen in Van Halen’s renowned video, “Van Halen: Hot for Teacher,” as young Michael Anthony Jr.

Yano is less active on social media. However, he has an Instagram account. As per the saying, opposite poles attract.

He barely posts on his social handles, unlike his wife. Both the husband and wife have an adorable family consisting of them and their pet dogs, whom they love dearly. Although they work in the same entertainment industry, the couple has never appeared together in any project. 

Yano Anaya Wife Selena Is A Professional Actress

Yano Anaya’s wife, Selena Anduze, is a professional actress and has worked in the same fraternity as her husband.

Selena Anduze has worked on notable projects as an actress and was recently featured in Jackie Ponder’s role in Hulu’s Candy television series. The show was based on crime, drama, and the biography of a housewife. Selena is a renowned face among the audience and has created a different personality among all the viewers. 

The actress was born and raised in the US Virgin Island of St. Croix and is the youngest of her parent’s three children. Selena belonged to French, Danish, Swedish, Puerto Rican, Native American Indian, Algerian, and Jewish descent and was born in a religious family. She has been a performing artist since age seven and was also the youngest performer to win the title of “Miss U.S. Virgin Islands” at the World Modeling Association talent awards in New York City at 9. 

To flourish her career as an actress and model, Selena then moved to the US as a teenager. She later performed in numerous regional theaters while completing her arts and sciences education studies. Selena eventually graduated with a Bachelor of Arts from Wesleyan University and a Master/Ph.D. in Health Sciences from Clayton College. Also, she held certifications in healing arts and writing.
