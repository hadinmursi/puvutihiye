Did Joaquin Phoenix and Rooney Mara Meet On Her? How Long Have Rooney Mara And Joaquin Phoenix Been

August 2024 · 2 minute read

It was on the set of the 2012 film “Her” that two of Hollywood’s finest actors, Joaquin Phoenix and Rooney Mara, first crossed paths. Little did they know that their initial interactions would lead to a blossoming romance that has stood the test of time.

At the time, Mara played Phoenix’s ex-wife in the movie, and while their on-screen chemistry was evident, their off-screen connection took some time to develop. In a candid interview with Vanity Fair in 2019, Phoenix revealed that he believed Mara didn’t like him at all during the filming of “Her.” However, he later discovered that Mara was just a bit shy, and her interest in him was more than apparent.

Their friendship evolved into something deeper, and the couple made their first public appearance together as a couple in May 2017 at the Cannes Film Festival. From there, their love story continued to flourish, and they took the next step by moving in together in the Hollywood Hills just four months later. Inside their home, they found solace in each other’s company, enjoying activities like exercising, studying lines, and binge-watching true crime documentaries on Netflix.

While the couple’s love for each other is evident, they have always chosen to keep their relationship away from the prying eyes of the media. Despite their low-key approach, rumors of an engagement began to circulate when Mara was spotted wearing a diamond ring in Los Angeles. However, insiders revealed that marriage wasn’t in the couple’s immediate plans, as they felt that the institution of marriage was too mainstream for their unconventional tastes.

Nevertheless, Joaquin Phoenix surprised everyone when he proposed to Rooney Mara in July 2019, confirming the engagement news that fans had eagerly awaited. Since then, the couple has been inseparable, supporting each other through thick and thin. Whether it’s film premieres, political rallies, or awards seasons, Mara has stood proudly by her soon-to-be husband’s side.

Their relationship is unique, as they share a commitment to holistic living, veganism, and a passion for making the world a better place. Despite not conforming to societal norms like marriage, their love and dedication to each other speak volumes about the strength of their bond.

As Joaquin Phoenix and Rooney Mara continue to build their life together, their love story remains an inspiring reminder that sometimes, the most beautiful connections can come from unexpected places. With their hearts intertwined, these two talented actors have found in each other a true partner in life and love.
