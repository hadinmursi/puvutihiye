Early Life & Biography

Kevin was born on October 5, 1988, and he was born in Owensboro, Kentucky, and his father was a Nigerian physiatrist whose name was Oluwole Olusola, and his mother was a Grenadian named Curlline Paul. Kevin started his education with the Greater Philadelphia Junior Academy, but then his family moved to Owensboro, which is in Kentucky, and there he was raised. He later attended the Triplett School, which was based in Montessori. Then he got transferred to the Owensboro Public School system for their accelerated math program, and this allowed him to take precalculus at the beginning of the 9th grade. Then he enrolled in the Phillips Academy Andover in the 11th grade.

After he graduated from Andover, he went to Yale University. He planned to pursue medicine at Yale, and he finished his pre-med requirements. He then started as an academic music major, and then he decided to switch to the East Asian Studies after he was introduced to China through a 10-day Chinese trip sponsored by Government for 100 Yale students. He was also the Director of Communication for the Rhodes Scholar led non-profit, which was known as College Outreach, and he also worked as a book monitor for the Yale Law School Library and also as a practice room monitor while he was at Yale School of Music. Later he graduated from Yale in the year 2011.
