Tshala Muana Biography and Wiki

Tshala Muana is an acclaimed Congo-Kinshasan Singer and Dancer who was born and brought up in Lubumbashi, Katanga, DRC. She began her artistic career in 1977 as a dancer for the musical band Tsheke Tsheke Love.

She is famous for several songs such as Karibu Yangu. She has received several awards on the national, continental and global scene. Tshala Muana was born to a soldier father named Amadeus Muidikayi and Alphonsine Bambiwa Tumba who was a housewife together with her 9 siblings.
