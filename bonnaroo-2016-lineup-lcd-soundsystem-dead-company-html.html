2016 Bonnaroo Lineup: Pearl Jam, LCD Soundsystem, Dead &amp; Company, and Flushable Toilets

August 2024 · 1 minute read
Encore. Already.

Because one major reunion stop is just not enough, LCD Soundsystem are also taking their restored act to this summer’s Bonnaroo. The Tennessee-based megafest on Tuesday unveiled its initial 2016 slate, which also includes the likes of Pearl Jam, Grateful Dead offshoot Dead & Company (another reunion, which comes with two sets), J. Cole, Tame Impala, Haim, CHVRCHES, Big Grams, Father John Misty, Tyler the Creator, Vince Staples, Third Eye Blind, and Shamir, among many, many others and soon-to-be additions. Here’s a poster:
